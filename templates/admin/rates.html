About Rates Management

Manage CD rates and ECR rates directly from the database. You can view, edit, add, and delete rates for all banks.

  • CD Rates: Certificate of Deposit rates by term and bank
  • ECR Rates: Excess Cash Reserve rates by bank and product type

Changes are saved immediately to the database and will be reflected in optimization calculations.
